about_title: "About the Network"
about_body: |
  The **Dutch Causal Inference Network** is an informal research community
  bringing together statisticians, methodologists, and domain scientists from
  universities across the Netherlands.

  We meet **once a quarter** to share work-in-progress, discuss methodology,
  and build connections across institutions. Meetings alternate between host
  universities and typically include one or two talks followed by informal
  discussion.

  Our shared interests include — but are not limited to:

  - Causal discovery and graphical models
  - Sensitivity analysis for observational studies
  - Instrumental variables, regression discontinuity, and related designs
  - Causal inference in high-dimensional and machine-learning settings
  - Applications in epidemiology, economics, and the social sciences

  Everyone working on or curious about causal inference is welcome to join.
  If you would like to attend a meeting, give a talk, or host a session at
  your institution, please reach out via the contact section below.
