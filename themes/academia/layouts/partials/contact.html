Want to join, present, or host a meeting?

The network is open to anyone working on or interested in causal inference at a Dutch university or research institute. There are no membership requirements — just show up.

To get in touch:

  • Mailing list — send an email to info@causalinference.nl and ask to be added. You will receive announcements for upcoming meetings.
  • Give a talk — reach out to any of the people listed above, or email the list directly. Talks are typically 30–45 minutes plus discussion.
  • Host a meeting — if your institution would like to host a quarterly meeting, please get in touch. We rotate between universities and are always looking for new venues.
